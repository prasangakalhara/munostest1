"summer is calling" collection

Summer is Calling" is a vibrant and playful NFT collection featuring the beloved black cat character in various summertime scenarios. Each NFT in this collection features a unique image of the black cat, bringing to life scenes of beach parties, tropical vacations, and more.

Roadmap

These NFTs are designed to capture the essence of summer fun and relaxation, with a touch of humor and whimsy. Whether lounging on a beach towel with a fruity cocktail, riding a wave on a surfboard, or playing music at a beach party, the black cat is always the life of the party. Each NFT in the "Summer is Calling" collection is a unique and one-of-a-kind artwork, created using the latest AI techniques to bring the black cat character to life. Collectors can add these NFTs to their collections, display them on their digital walls, or trade them with other collectors. With their vibrant colors, playful themes, and memorable character, the NFTs in the "Summer is Calling" collection are sure to be a hit among fans of digital art and meme culture. So answer the call of summer and add a black cat NFT to your collection today!

Phase 1

Concept and Design

Define the overall concept and theme for the collection (summer fun and relaxation with black cat)

Develop the initial designs and concepts for the NFTs, including different scenes and poses for the black cat character

Refine the designs based on feedback from internal team and potential customers

Phase 2

Marketing and Promotion

Develop a marketing and promotion strategy, including social media outreach, email marketing, and influencer collaborations

Build a landing page or website to showcase the NFT collection and provide information on how to purchase and collect them

Create a teaser campaign to generate buzz and excitement before the launch

Phase 3

Launch and Sale

Set a launch date for the collection and create a countdown timer to build anticipation

Create scarcity and exclusivity by limiting the number of NFTs available for purchase and setting a price that reflects the value of the artwork

Launch the NFT collection and promote it heavily through social media and other marketing channels

Phase 4

After-Sale Support and Engagement

Provide ongoing support to customers who purchase the NFTs, including technical assistance and information on how to display and trade them

Encourage engagement and interaction with the NFTs through gamification or other interactive elements, such as a leaderboard or contests

Collect feedback from customers and use it to inform future collections and design decisions
